Who are we?

Celestia Reign is a South Korean girl group formed by Stellar Sound Entertainment. The group is composed of four members: Sora, Ryuu, Luna and Kai. Their debut album, "Watercolor skies" introduces a unique sound that’s garnering attention both in Asia and internationally.

Celestia Reign is a K-pop/J-pop fusion band that promotes a unique blend of genres, including pop, R&B and electronic elements. Their product offerings include physical albums, exclusive digital content, concert tickets, VIP packages, digital fan clubs with early access to merchandise, pre-releases, and interactive experiences.

Our Music

Our debut album, “Celestia Reign” invites you into the world of Eternal. It’s a universe filled with mystery, anticipation, and the thrill of discovery. Each track is a story—a celestial tale of wonder, longing, and adventure. With a mix of soaring vocals, hypnotic instrumentals, and dynamic production, the album captures the essence of what it means to reach for the stars.

A Visual Odyssey

Our visuals are as important as the music we create. Inspired by space, myth, and fantasy, the imagery of Celestial Reign transports you to new worlds, reflecting the infinite possibilities we seek to express.
